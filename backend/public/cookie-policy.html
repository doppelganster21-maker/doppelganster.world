1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and actions over time. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

3. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Check your browser's help documentation for instructions.
  • Google Analytics Opt-out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Google Ad Settings: Visit Google Ad Settings to manage personalised advertising.
  • Our Cookie Banner: When you first visit Doppelganger.world, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies via our banner.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of this website.
