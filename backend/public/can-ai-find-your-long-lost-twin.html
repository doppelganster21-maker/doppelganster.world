What is a Twin Stranger?

A twin stranger is someone who looks identical to you but is not biologically related. The concept of twin strangers has been around for centuries, but with the advancement of AI and face recognition technology, it is now possible to find your twin stranger online.

Types of Twin Strangers

  • Biological twins: identical or fraternal twins who share the same DNA
  • Twin strangers: people who look identical but are not biologically related

How Does AI Face Recognition Work?

AI face recognition technology uses advanced algorithms to analyze and compare facial features. The process involves:

  1. Face detection: detecting the face in an image or video
  2. Face alignment: aligning the face to a standard position
  3. Feature extraction: extracting unique facial features
  4. Comparison: comparing the extracted features with a database of known faces

Can AI Find Your Biological Family?

Yes, AI can help find your biological family by analyzing your DNA and comparing it with a database of known DNA profiles. This is done through:

  • DNA testing: analyzing your DNA to create a unique profile
