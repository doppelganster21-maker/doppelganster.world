Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding your doppelganger and using our platform.

How does Doppelganger.world find my look-alike?

Our AI analyses 68+ facial landmark points from your uploaded portrait photo — including eye spacing, nose bridge, jawline, and lip proportions. These landmarks are converted into a unique facial vector that is compared against our global database of face submissions. Results are ranked by cosine similarity score, showing you the closest matches first.

How accurate is the face-matching algorithm?

Our matching engine achieves 92%+ precision on clear, front-facing portrait photos. Accuracy decreases with low lighting, extreme angles, heavy filters, or partial face coverage. For best results, use a natural-light, front-facing photo with no sunglasses or heavy makeup.

What photo formats are supported?

We accept JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP formats. Maximum file size is 10MB. The photo must clearly show the full face. Group photos or photos where the face is less than 20% of the frame may not process correctly.

How long does a face scan take?

Most scans complete in 5–15 seconds. During peak traffic, results may take up to 60 seconds. You'll see a progress indicator while the AI is processing your photo.

Is my photo stored permanently?

Photos uploaded for scanning only are processed in memory and are not permanently stored unless you explicitly submit your photo to the public gallery or post a bounty. If you choose to submit to the gallery, your photo may be retained for matching purposes. You can request deletion at any time by emailing contact@doppelganger.world.

Is my facial data sold to third parties?

No. We do not sell, rent, or trade your biometric or personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share anonymised, aggregated statistics with analytics partners. Please read our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do cookies work on this site?

We use cookies to improve your experience, enable core functionality (login, preferences), and serve personalised ads via Google AdSense. You can manage cookie preferences at any time. See our Cookie Policy for a full breakdown.

What is a look-alike bounty?

A bounty is a reward posted by a user who wants to find their real-life doppelganger. They upload their photo, set a cash or prize reward (e.g. ₹10,000), and our community of look-alike hunters can submit verified matches to claim the prize. It's a fun, gamified way to connect real-world twins.

How do I post a bounty?

Sign in with your Google account, navigate to the Gallery section on the homepage, and click Post a Bounty. Fill in the reward details, upload your photo, and publish. Your bounty will be visible to all users.

How are bounty matches verified?

Submitted matches are reviewed by our AI similarity engine (threshold: 85%+) and optionally reviewed by the bounty poster. A match is confirmed only when both the AI score meets the threshold and the poster approves. This prevents fraudulent claims.

Is Doppelganger.world free to use?

Basic face scanning is completely free. You can upload a photo and receive look-alike matches at no cost. Premium features — such as high-precision deep scans, advanced filters, and posting bounties — may be subject to fees. We'll always be transparent about any paid features before you proceed.

Do I need to create an account to use the site?

No account is required for a basic face scan. However, to participate in bounties, save your scan history, or submit to the gallery, you'll need to sign in with Google. This keeps your data secure and ensures only genuine users interact with the platform.

How do I delete my account and data?

Email contact@doppelganger.world with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our team via the Contact page or email us directly at contact@doppelganger.world. We typically respond within 24–48 business hours.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24–48 hours.
